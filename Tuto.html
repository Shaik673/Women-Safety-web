Reporting and Support: Resources and Procedures for Addressing Safety Threats


1

Contact Emergency Services

Dial 911 or your local emergency

number if you are in imediate danger.

2

Report to Authorities

File a police report or contact

a local sexual assault or domestic

violence hotline to seek support

and justice for all womens.

3

Seek Medical Attention

If you have been injured,

seek immediate medical attention

to address any physical injuries

and recieve necessary care.

4

Connect with Support Groups

Join a support group or seek

counseling to process your

experience and find a community

of understanding.
